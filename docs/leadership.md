# Leadership Section Bios & Tone Rules

This document details the editorial voice, bio formatting, and commentary standards for the Leadership page.

---

## 1. Section Purpose & Mandate
The [leadership.html](file:///Users/aravindsaj/Library/CloudStorage/GoogleDrive-ask5615@g.rit.edu/My%20Drive/DeskEssentials/Systems/Insight%20Website/leadership.html) section hosts executive statements, annual founder letters, and strategic CEO insights. It establishes the philosophical foundation of Aoura Group's long-term operations.

---

## 2. Leadership Tone of Voice
Communications from leadership must adhere strictly to these criteria:
- **Tone**: Pragmatic, data-grounded, humble, and analytical.
- **Perspective**: Focus on 5-to-10-year horizons rather than quarter-to-quarter hype.
- **Language Avoidance**: Eliminate marketing buzzwords (e.g., *"disruption"*, *"revolutionary"*). Discuss logistics in terms of operational efficiencies, fulfillment speeds, capital yields, and capacity multipliers.

---

## 3. Executive Biography Formatting
Any profile or bio rendered for Aoura Group leaders must include:
- **Full Name & Title**: Clean serif/sans-serif typography.
- **Tenure & Background**: Focused on industry, distribution, logistics, and capital management experience.
- **Writing Portfolio**: A list of links to historical letters (e.g., *"Founder's Letter 2026"*).
- **Portrait Photo Rules**: Black and white, high-contrast portraits, matching the minimal corporate aesthetic.
