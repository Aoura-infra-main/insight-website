Strategic Expansion and Vision

Dubai, UAE — June 23, 2026 — Aoura Group is pleased to announce the acquisition of Alphalake Technologies, which will now operate under the new name Aoura Technologies as part of the Group's long-term technology and innovation strategy.

The acquisition marks a significant milestone in Aoura Group's vision to expand its digital capabilities and build proprietary software solutions that enhance operational efficiency, business intelligence, and customer experiences across its growing portfolio of companies.

Investment and Product Roadmap

As part of this strategic move, Aoura Group plans to invest substantially in Aoura Technologies to accelerate research and development, expand engineering resources, and create innovative software products for both internal business operations and external commercial markets.

Aoura Technologies will focus on developing solutions in business automation, e-commerce infrastructure, procurement systems, enterprise resource planning, analytics, and workflow management. Several software initiatives are currently in development and are expected to support organizations across multiple industries.

Leadership and Market Impact

"The acquisition of Alphalake Technologies represents an important step in our long-term commitment to innovation," said an Aoura Group spokesperson. "Technology is becoming increasingly important across every sector we operate in, and Aoura Technologies will serve as the foundation for developing scalable software solutions that drive efficiency, growth, and digital transformation."

The investment will support talent acquisition, cloud infrastructure, cybersecurity initiatives, product development, and the exploration of emerging technologies that can deliver measurable value to businesses and consumers alike.

As Aoura Group's dedicated technology division, Aoura Technologies will work closely with the Group's operating companies while also developing standalone software products for broader market adoption.

Additional announcements regarding upcoming product launches, technology partnerships, and platform releases are expected later this year.
