Overview & Migration

Aoura Group is proud to officially unveil Aoura Store, our completely reimagined and unified B2B e-commerce platform. Designed to provide a seamless procurement experience for our commercial partners across the UAE and beyond, the new storefront is now live at store.aouragrp.com.

Please note that as part of this transition, our legacy platform at shop.aouragrp.com is no longer active. All customer accounts, order histories, and inventories have been securely migrated to the new Aoura Store ecosystem.

Powered by Aoura Technology

This major upgrade was developed entirely in collaboration with Aoura Technology. By leveraging their expertise in building scalable, secure, and lightning-fast digital infrastructures, the new Aoura Store features real-time inventory tracking, streamlined bulk-ordering interfaces, and advanced filtering options to help our partners find exactly what they need instantly.

Extensive Product Portfolio

The new Aoura Store is the definitive source for all Aoura Products and partnered brands. Our expansive catalog currently features thousands of items across key industrial and commercial categories, including:

  • Packaging & Shipping: Aoura Bubble Pouches (available in various sizes) and Heavy Duty 5-Ply Corrugated Carton Boxes for secure transit and storage.
  • Industrial Products: High-precision Laser Protective Lenses (1064nm), premium Masking Tapes, Clear Packing Tapes, Screws & Nails Assortments, Tital Cutting Discs, Jumlee Utility Knives, Lonical Measuring Tapes, and TECHWELD Clear Welding Glass.
  • Labels & Barcodes: AOURA Thermal Receipt Paper Rolls and Direct Thermal Barcode Labels designed for modern POS systems.
  • Office & Paper Supplies: High-quality DELI Laminator Machines, alongside premium A-Grade paper reams from Evernes, NIPL, Double A, JK Copier, BINDALS, MIILK, and HP Everyday.
  • Safety & PPE: Flame-resistant coveralls, ComfortMax and PetroKing safety shoes from Red Wing, WELDMAN Welding Face Shields and Gloves, and RIGMAN Work Gloves.
  • Hospitality Supplies: Al Fajr Facial Tissues and resort-grade AOURA 600 GSM Cotton Bath Towels and Wash Cloths.

We invite all our partners to explore the new platform and experience the future of B2B procurement. Visit store.aouragrp.com today to place your next order.
