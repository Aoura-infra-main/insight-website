HOLDING STRUCTURE

Operating Companies

A deep dive into the business divisions, histories, operational models, and strategic positions within the Aoura Group ecosystem.

HOLDING PARENT

Aoura Group

Overview

Aoura Group is a professional UAE-based holding company focused on commerce, physical distribution systems, and e-commerce operational infrastructure. The group acts as a parental framework overseeing specialized operating divisions that help brands scale both offline and online channels throughout the GCC.

History

Founded in the UAE, Aoura Group was structured to eliminate fragmentation in corporate supply procurement and digital marketplace operations. By consolidating these dual streams under one holding banner, the group enabled specialized execution under separate operational managements while sharing centralized corporate resources.

Mission

To build the foundational physical and digital infrastructure that empowers brands to launch, manage, and scale distribution operations seamlessly across the UAE and GCC region.

Operations

Centralized capital allocation, board-level corporate strategy oversight, joint compliance auditing, legal structuring, and proprietary software resource sharing across all active subsidiaries.

Markets Served

United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, Oman, and international product brands seeking localized commercial entry to the Gulf markets.

Strategic Direction

Scaling distribution assets, building localized software solutions to consolidate wholesale inventory procurement, and expanding logistics capabilities to support larger corporate integrations.

DIVISION 01

Aoura Products

Overview

Aoura Products is the distribution and supply division of Aoura Group. The business functions as a B2B wholesale supplier, serving offices, warehouses, logistics operators, retailers, manufacturers, and industrial accounts.

History

Launched to resolve procurement complexities for corporate offices and logistics facilities, Aoura Products expanded quickly from supplying basic shipping consumables into a comprehensive distributor of B2B industrial warehouse materials and office procurement solutions.

Mission

To become the premier B2B wholesale supply and logistics partner in the UAE, providing friction-free procurement of industrial, warehouse, and office materials.

Operations

Large-scale warehousing, inventory management, logistics fulfillment, next-day shipping fleet operations, and bulk wholesale catalog curation spanning packaging, safety, and business supplies.

Markets Served

Corporate offices, warehouse operations, logistics providers, retail chains, and manufacturing facilities across all seven emirates of the UAE.

Strategic Direction

Expanding fulfillment hubs, consolidating supplier partnerships to increase wholesale bulk discounts, and integrating electric logistics vehicles into regional distribution systems.

DIVISION 02

Aoura E-commerce Services

Overview

Aoura E-commerce Services is the marketplace infrastructure division of the group, specialized in helping brands launch, optimize, and scale online operations on major regional platforms.

History

Established to meet the rapid e-commerce expansion in the UAE and wider GCC, the service was structured to help brands navigate localized marketplace rules, search algorithm optimizations, and regional fulfillment channels.

Mission

To build standard-setting digital commerce solutions that enable international and local brands to maximize market share on Amazon UAE and Noon.

Operations

Amazon UAE & Noon marketplace account management, product search optimization (SEO), inventory forecasting, listing design, advertising campaign management, and compliance advisory.

Markets Served

UAE and GCC digital marketplaces, supporting regional manufacturers and global brands seeking to expand their online footprint in the Middle East.

Strategic Direction

Integrating AI-driven pricing engines, expanding listing optimization models, and offering deeper multi-channel fulfillment advisory for fast-moving consumer goods.
